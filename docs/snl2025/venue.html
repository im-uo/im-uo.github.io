Ninth International Workshop on Symbolic-Neural Learning (SNL2025)

October 29-30, 2025
Nakanoshima Center 10F, The University of Osaka (Osaka, Japan)

Venue

The workshop will be located at the Nakanoshima Center, The Univeristy of Osaka.

4-3-53 Nakanoshima, Kita-ward, Osaka-city, Osaka, 530-0005 Japan

The registration desk is located on the 10th floor. Please directly come to the 10th floor with the elevator.

Access by Train (Machine Translation)

  • Keihan Nakanoshima Line: about 5 minutes on foot from Nakanoshima Station
  • Hanshin Main Line: about 9 minutes on foot from Fukushima Station
  • JR Tozai Line: about 9 minutes on foot from Shin-Fukushima Station
  • JR Loop Line: about 12 minutes on foot from Fukushima Station
  • Osaka Metro Yotsubashi Line: about 10 minutes on foot from Higobashi Station
  • Osaka Metro Midosuji Line: about 16 minutes on foot from Yodoyabashi Station

Access by Bus (Machine Translation)

  • Osaka City Bus (Route 53): From Osaka Station Bus Terminal → get off at Nakanoshima 4-chome (Former Tamaebashi), 1 min on foot
  • Osaka City Bus (Route 75): From Osaka Station Bus Terminal → get off at Taminobashi, 2 mins on foot
  • Hokko Bus (Nakanoshima Loop Bus “Furara”): From JR Osaka Station West Exit → get off at Nakanoshima Museum of Art, Osaka, 2 mins on foot
